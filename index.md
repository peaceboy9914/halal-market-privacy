# Privacy Policy for Halal Market

**Last Updated:** December 11, 2025  
**Effective Date:** December 11, 2025

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Halal Market ("we," "our," or "us"). Halal Market is a multi-vendor e-commerce marketplace application designed for the Ethiopian market. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and protect your information.

This Privacy Policy explains:
- What information we collect
- How we use your information
- How we share your information
- Your rights and choices
- How we protect your information
- Contact information

By using Halal Market, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

#### Account Information
- **Vendors**: When you sign in with Google Sign-In, we collect:
  - Your Google account email address
  - Your name and profile picture (from Google account)
  - Your phone number (if provided during store setup)
  
- **Investors**: When you register for Islamic finance services, we collect:
  - Name
  - Email address
  - Phone number
  - Deposit and investment information

- **Employees**: When you register for the referral program, we collect:
  - Name
  - Email address
  - Phone number
  - Referral code and referral activity

#### Store and Business Information (Vendors)
- Store name and description
- Physical location (region, city, woreda, specific address)
- Phone number (required)
- Store images (logo, banner, photos)
- Business hours
- Store category

#### Product Information (Vendors)
- Product name, description, and price
- Product images (up to 5 images per product)
- Product category and subcategory
- Stock quantity
- Payment receipts for product listing fees (1% of product price)

#### Cart and Wishlist Data
- Products added to cart (for reference only - no online checkout)
- Products added to wishlist
- Cart persists for 30 days

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

#### Device Information
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- Unique device identifiers
- IP address
- Mobile network information

#### Location Information
- **Precise Location**: We collect your precise location (GPS coordinates) when you:
  - Enable location services for vendor/product discovery
  - Search for products or vendors near you
  - Filter products by location
  
- **Approximate Location**: We may collect approximate location based on:
  - IP address
  - Network information

**Note**: Location services are optional. You can disable location permissions in your device settings, but some features may not work properly.

#### Usage and Analytics Data
We use Firebase Analytics to collect information about how you use the app:
- App usage patterns and screen views
- Features you interact with
- Search queries
- Products you view
- Cart and wishlist actions
- Phone call initiations (to vendors or call center)
- Time spent in the app
- Error logs and crash reports

This data is collected automatically and helps us improve the app experience.

### 2.3 Information from Third-Party Services

#### Google Sign-In
- When you sign in with Google, we receive:
  - Your Google account email
  - Your name
  - Your profile picture URL
  - Your Google account ID

This information is used solely for authentication and account creation.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

### 3.1 Core App Functionality
- **Account Management**: Create and manage your user account
- **Authentication**: Verify your identity using Google Sign-In
- **Store Management**: Enable vendors to set up and manage their stores
- **Product Management**: Enable vendors to list and manage products
- **Product Discovery**: Help customers find products and vendors
- **Location-Based Services**: Show products and vendors near you
- **Cart and Wishlist**: Save your cart items and wishlist for reference

### 3.2 Communication
- **Vendor Contact**: Display vendor phone numbers so customers can call directly
- **Call Center**: Enable customers to call the call center for delivery orders
- **Notifications**: Send push notifications about:
  - Product approvals (vendors)
  - Store approvals (vendors)
  - Order updates (if applicable)
  - Platform announcements

### 3.3 Business Operations
- **Product Listing Fees**: Process and verify payment receipts for product listings (1% of product price)
- **Admin Review**: Enable administrators to review and approve stores and products
- **Referral System**: Track and manage referral bonuses for employees
- **Islamic Finance**: Manage deposits and investments (if applicable)

### 3.4 Analytics and Improvement
- **App Analytics**: Understand how users interact with the app
- **Performance Monitoring**: Identify and fix bugs and crashes
- **Feature Development**: Improve existing features and develop new ones
- **User Experience**: Personalize and optimize the app experience

### 3.5 Legal and Security
- **Compliance**: Comply with applicable laws and regulations
- **Security**: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security threats
- **Dispute Resolution**: Resolve disputes and enforce our terms

## 4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share your information only in the following circumstances:

### 4.1 Public Information
- **Vendor Profiles**: Store names, descriptions, locations, and contact information are visible to all users
- **Product Listings**: Product information (name, description, price, images) is visible to all users
- **Store Locations**: Store addresses are visible to help customers find vendors

### 4.2 Service Providers
We use third-party services that may have access to your information:
- **Google Firebase**: For authentication, database, storage, analytics, and messaging
- **Google Analytics**: For app analytics and usage tracking
- **Cloud Storage**: For storing images and files (via Firebase Storage)

These service providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and only use your information to provide services to us.

### 4.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to:
- Court orders or legal processes
- Government requests
- Law enforcement investigations
- Protection of rights, property, or safety

### 4.4 Business Transfers
If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

## 5. Data Storage and Security

### 5.1 Data Storage
- **Location**: Your data is stored on Google Firebase servers, which may be located outside Ethiopia
- **Retention**: We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services
- **Cart Data**: Cart items are retained for 30 days
- **Deletion**: You can request deletion of your account and data (see Section 7)

### 5.2 Security Measures
We implement security measures to protect your information:
- **Encryption**: Data transmitted between the app and servers is encrypted
- **Authentication**: Secure authentication via Google Sign-In
- **Access Controls**: Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
- **Firebase Security**: Firebase provides enterprise-grade security

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 6. Third-Party Services

### 6.2 Location Services

## 7. Your Rights and Choices

### 7.1 Access and Correction
- **View Your Data**: You can view your account information, store details, and product listings in the app
- **Update Your Data**: You can update your profile, store information, and product listings at any time
- **Delete Your Data**: You can request deletion of your account and data by contacting us (see Section 10)

### 7.2 Location Services
- **Enable/Disable**: You can enable or disable location services in your device settings
- **Impact**: Disabling location services may limit location-based features

### 7.3 Analytics
- **Opt-Out**: You can opt out of analytics data collection by:
  - Disabling analytics in your device settings (if available)
  - Contacting us to disable analytics for your account

### 7.4 Account Deletion
- **Request Deletion**: You can request deletion of your account and all associated data
- **Process**: Contact us (see Section 10) to request account deletion
- **Timeline**: We will process deletion requests within 30 days
- **Note**: Some information may be retained for legal or business purposes

### 7.5 Uninstall
- You can uninstall the app at any time
- Uninstalling the app will remove it from your device but will not delete your account or data
- To delete your account and data, you must contact us (see Section 10)

## 8. Children's Privacy

Halal Market is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately, and we will delete that information.

## 9. International Data Transfers
