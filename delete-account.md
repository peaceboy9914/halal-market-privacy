# Request Account Deletion - Halal Market

## How to Delete Your Account

To delete your Halal Market account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

1. **Send an email** to: mahdinurazaman@gmail.com
2. **Subject line**: "Account Deletion Request"
3. **Include in your email**:
   - Your account email address
   - Your name
   - Reason for deletion (optional)

We will process your request within **30 days**.

## What Gets Deleted

When you request account deletion, we will permanently delete:
- Your user account and authentication data
- Your profile information (name, email, phone number)
- Your store information (if you're a vendor)
- Your product listings (if you're a vendor)
- Your cart and wishlist data
- Your location data
- Your usage and analytics data

## What May Be Retained

Some information may be retained for:
- **Legal compliance**: As required by applicable laws and regulations
- **Business record-keeping**: Financial records, transaction history (as required by law)
- **Fraud prevention**: Security and fraud prevention purposes
- **Dispute resolution**: Information related to ongoing disputes or legal proceedings

## Additional Information

- Uninstalling the app does NOT delete your account or data
- You must contact us to request account deletion
- Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
- We will confirm deletion via email once completed
